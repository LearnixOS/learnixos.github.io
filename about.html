LearnixOS, also known as LXOS, is a Linux distro made by LearnixTV’s community with the aim to aid users who are new to Linux in trying out a source-based distro.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and customizable Linux experience that caters to both beginners and advanced users. We strive to make Linux accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

Join our community and be part of the LearnixOS journey!
