About LearniXOS

Discover LearniXOS, a Linux distribution crafted for education, experimentation, and open-source collaboration.

Our Mission

Learn about the vision and goals behind LearniXOS.

LearniXOS is a Linux distribution crafted for those who want to learn about operating systems, experiment with Linux, and contribute to an open-source project.

Our mission is to provide a lightweight, flexible, and educational platform for students, developers, and enthusiasts.

Join our community to explore our goals, access documentation, and get involved in shaping the future of LearniXOS.
